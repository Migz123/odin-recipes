Chocolate Hazelnut Fruit Crepes

Description

Chocolate Hazelnut Fruit Crepes are a french dish that can be had anytime of day. Very sweet and can be made anyway you want inside the crepe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 4 (7 inch) pre-made crepes
  • 4 bananas, sliced
  • 1 (7 ounce) can pressurized whipped cream

Steps

  1. Spread 1/4 cup of chocolate hazelnut spread onto each crepe.
  2. Arrange 1 sliced banana down the center of each one.
  3. Roll up, and place in a warm skillet over medium heat.
  4. Let them warm up for about 90 seconds.
  5. Transfer to plates, and serve topped with whipped cream.
